# Governance

Nami is currently a **founder-led** project: final decisions rest with the maintainer(s) listed in [MAINTAINERS.md](MAINTAINERS.md).

## How decisions are made

- **Architecture decisions** are recorded as ADRs in [`docs/adr/`](docs/adr/) with context, options considered, and rationale. Accepted ADRs are binding until superseded by a new ADR.
- **Feature direction** follows the public roadmap in the README. Proposals are welcome via discussions/issues.
- **Code changes** merge via pull request review by a maintainer.

## Becoming a maintainer

Sustained, high-quality contributions (code, review, triage, docs) lead to commit rights by invitation of the existing maintainers. The bar is trust and judgment, not volume.

## Evolution

As the contributor base grows, governance will evolve toward a documented multi-maintainer model; changes to this document are announced in release notes.
